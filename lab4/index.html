Lab 4 - Pseudocoding and Problem-solving

Challenge

Get in the habit of thinking in terms of pseudocoding before jumping into coding. With your partner, pseudocode an everyday task most students may not be familiar with. Examples include, changing a tire, making salsa, baking bread, getting an A in a class, or making art.

Problems

Trying to figure out which game to do pseudocode for and the steps needed for it. Trying to figure out how to format the assignment and what exactly to submit.

Reflection

This assignment was kinda difficult at first when it came to picking a topic to write pseudocode for. Overall it was a simple assignment to follow and did not run into too many problems. I mainly used wmodes.github.io/art101/lab4/index.html for help on this assignment.
